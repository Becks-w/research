Comparative Overview of 17 Elite AI Investors
17位顶级人工智能投资人对比概览
This report profiles 17 of the most closely watched investors, operators, and allocators at the intersection of artificial intelligence and capital markets. They span four archetypes: AI lab insiders who possess technical foresight from building frontier models; AI-focused VCs who deploy early-stage capital into the application and infrastructure layers; macro hedge fund managers who use public market positioning and 13F filings to signal large-scale thematic conviction; and tech founder-investors whose operating experience in building technology companies at scale informs both their strategic commentary and their capital allocation.

OpenAI & AI Lab Alumni

Leopold Aschenbrenner
利奥波德·阿申布伦纳
Founder, Situational Awareness Fund
AI Lab Alum Macro/Infra
Energy Compute Defence Bitcoin Miners
Born around 2001, Leopold Aschenbrenner graduated as valedictorian from Columbia University in 2021 at the age of 19, double-majoring in economics and mathematics-statistics — one of the youngest valedictorians in the university's modern history. While at Columbia, he co-founded the university's effective altruism chapter, an early signal of his deep engagement with long-term risk and civilizational-scale thinking. He briefly worked at the philanthropy arm of Sam Bankman-Fried's FTX exchange before pivoting to AI research.
中文摘要
阿申布伦纳约生于2001年，2021年以19岁高龄以年级第一身份毕业于哥伦比亚大学，主修经济学与数学统计，是该校近代史上最年轻的毕业致辞代表之一。在校期间，他联合创立了校内有效利他主义俱乐部，展现出其对长期风险与文明尺度议题的深度关注。他曾短暂供职于FTX旗下的慈善机构，后转型从事AI研究。
Aschenbrenner joined OpenAI in 2023 as a researcher on the Superalignment team, a group tasked with developing technical methods to control AI systems more powerful than humans. He authored an internal memo warning OpenAI's board that the company's security protocols were insufficient to prevent foreign espionage — a warning that created friction with leadership. He was fired in April 2024, an event he publicly disputed. Rather than retreat, he converted the dismissal into a platform: in June 2024, he self-published the landmark "Situational Awareness: The Decade Ahead" — a 165-page essay series that became required reading in Silicon Valley and positioned him as the defining voice on AGI timelines and geopolitical AI risk.
中文摘要
阿申布伦纳于2023年加入OpenAI，任职于超级对齐团队，该团队专注于研究如何控制比人类更强大的AI系统。他撰写了一份内部备忘录，警告OpenAI董事会公司的安全协议不足以防范外国间谍活动，此举在内部引发摩擦。2024年4月他遭到解雇，对此他公开表示异议。然而他将这次解雇转化为发声平台：2024年6月，他自行发布了里程碑式的《情境意识：未来十年》——165页的系列文章，迅速成为硅谷必读文本，使其成为AGI时间线与地缘政治AI风险领域最具影响力的声音。
Backed by Nat Friedman, Daniel Gross, and Patrick and John Collison (among others), Aschenbrenner launched the Situational Awareness Fund. By 2025 the fund controlled over $1.5 billion in assets; by February 2026 it had grown to $5.52 billion across 29 holdings. His core investment thesis is that the path to AGI is bottlenecked not by algorithms but by physical infrastructure: power generation, cooling, and silicon manufacturing. Key reported positions include Bloom Energy, CoreWeave, Lumentum, Bitcoin miners (IREN, Riot Platforms, Cipher Mining), and options on Intel — a portfolio that effectively bets that energy becomes the scarcest resource in the age of superintelligence.
中文摘要
在弗里德曼、格罗斯及科利森兄弟等人的支持下，阿申布伦纳创立了情境意识基金。至2025年，该基金管理资产超15亿美元；截至2026年2月，已增长至55.2亿美元，持有29只股票。其核心投资逻辑是：通往AGI的道路并不受制于算法，而是受制于物理基础设施——电力、散热与芯片制造。已披露的主要持仓包括Bloom Energy、CoreWeave、Lumentum、比特币矿商（IREN、Riot Platforms、Cipher Mining）以及英特尔期权——该投资组合实质上押注于"超级智能时代能源将成为最稀缺资源"这一主题。
Strengths · 优势
Insider knowledge of AI scaling dynamics. Rapid AUM growth validates thesis. Strong LP network (Collisons, Friedman, Gross).
Watch Risks · 风险
Young fund with limited public track record. AGI timeline uncertainty. Energy thesis depends on sustained capex cycles.
Andrej Karpathy
安德烈·卡帕西
Founder, Eureka Labs; Former Tesla Director of AI; OpenAI Co-Founder
AI Researcher Tech Signal
LLM Research Autonomous Vehicles AI Education
Born in Bratislava, Slovakia, Karpathy moved to Toronto at age 15. He completed a BSc in Computer Science and Physics at the University of Toronto (2009), an MSc at UBC (2011), and a PhD at Stanford in 2015 under Fei-Fei Li, focusing on the intersection of natural language processing and computer vision — one of the most prescient research directions of his generation. He was a founding research scientist at OpenAI from 2015 to 2017, working on deep learning, reinforcement learning, and generative modelling in the organisation's earliest days.
中文摘要
卡帕西生于斯洛伐克布拉迪斯拉发，15岁移居多伦多。他先后在多伦多大学取得计算机科学与物理学学士学位（2009年）、在英属哥伦比亚大学取得硕士学位（2011年），并于2015年在斯坦福大学师从李飞飞完成博士学业，研究方向为自然语言处理与计算机视觉的交叉领域，堪称其一代最具前瞻性的研究方向之一。他是OpenAI的创始研究科学家（2015—2017年），在该机构成立初期从事深度学习、强化学习与生成式建模工作。
At Tesla (2017–2022) as Director of Artificial Intelligence, Karpathy oversaw the transformation of the Autopilot system from a radar-and-camera hybrid into a pure vision-based neural network — a controversial but technically audacious decision that influenced the entire autonomous driving industry. After a brief return to OpenAI (2023–2024), he founded Eureka Labs in July 2024, an AI-native education company aimed at using AI teaching assistants to make expert-level education universally accessible. His income derives primarily from Tesla stock options earned during his tenure, advisory roles, and angel investments in AI companies.
中文摘要
在特斯拉担任人工智能总监（2017—2022年）期间，卡帕西主导了Autopilot系统从雷达摄像头混合方案向纯视觉神经网络的技术转型——这一颇具争议但技术上极为大胆的决策深刻影响了整个自动驾驶行业。短暂回归OpenAI后（2023—2024年），他于2024年7月创立了Eureka Labs，一家以人工智能教学助手为核心的AI原生教育公司，旨在让专家级教育触手可及。其收入主要来自特斯拉期权、顾问角色及AI公司天使投资。
Karpathy's value as a signal source is technical rather than financial. When he comments publicly on model capability milestones — e.g., his assessments of GPT-4, LLaMA, or training dynamics — those statements carry outsized weight because they come from someone who has trained frontier models at both OpenAI and Tesla. He was named to TIME's 100 Most Influential People in AI (2024). His free educational content (including the popular "Neural Networks: Zero to Hero" YouTube series) has become a primary on-ramp for the next generation of AI engineers.
中文摘要
卡帕西作为信号来源的价值在于技术层面而非财务层面。当他公开评论模型能力里程碑时——例如对GPT-4、LLaMA或训练动力学的判断——这些观点具有超乎寻常的参考价值，因为他是在OpenAI和特斯拉均训练过前沿模型的少数人之一。他入选《时代》2024年百位最具影响力AI人物。他的免费教育内容（包括广受欢迎的YouTube系列"神经网络：从零到英雄"）已成为新一代AI工程师的主要入门资源。
Nat Friedman
纳特·弗里德曼
Co-lead, Meta Superintelligence Labs; Former GitHub CEO; Co-founder, C2 Investments
AI Lab / VC Builder-Investor
Developer Tools Open-Source AI AI Compute
Nat Friedman holds a BS in Computer Science and Mathematics from MIT (1999). In 1999, he co-founded Ximian with Miguel de Icaza to build GNOME desktop applications, later founding Xamarin in 2011 — a cross-platform mobile development tools company that Microsoft acquired in 2016. These back-to-back operator exits established Friedman as a serious builder long before his VC career. He became CEO of GitHub in October 2018, where he oversaw one of the most consequential product launches in AI history: GitHub Copilot — the first widely adopted, commercially successful generative AI product. He stepped down in November 2021.
中文摘要
弗里德曼持有麻省理工学院计算机科学与数学学士学位（1999年）。1999年，他与米格尔·德·伊卡萨联合创立了Ximian，后于2011年创立了Xamarin——一家跨平台移动开发工具公司，于2016年被微软收购。连续两次创业退出奠定了他作为资深构建者的地位。2018年10月出任GitHub CEO，任内主导了AI史上最具影响力的产品发布之一：GitHub Copilot——第一个被广泛采用的商业化成功生成式AI产品。2021年11月卸任。
Alongside Daniel Gross, Friedman co-founded C2 Investments and the AI Grant programme, which provides $250,000 to AI-native startups. They also co-deployed the Andromeda Cluster in 2023 — a $100 million supercomputer of 2,512 NVIDIA H100 GPUs — directly to their portfolio companies, providing compute before most AI startups could access it commercially. Portfolio highlights include: Perplexity AI, Character.ai, Safe Superintelligence (SSI), HeyGen, Magic, Suno, Weights & Biases, Pika, and EvolutionaryScale. He was an LP in the Situational Awareness Fund and joined Meta Superintelligence Labs as co-lead in June 2025 alongside Alexandr Wang.
中文摘要
弗里德曼与丹尼尔·格罗斯联合创立了C2 Investments与AI Grant项目（为AI原生初创公司提供25万美元资助）。二人还于2023年联合部署了仙女座集群——一台耗资约1亿美元、配备2512块英伟达H100 GPU的超级计算机，直接向其投资组合公司提供算力。主要投资包括：Perplexity AI、Character.ai、Safe Superintelligence（SSI）、HeyGen、Magic、Suno、Weights & Biases、Pika及EvolutionaryScale。他是情境意识基金的LP，并于2025年6月与王仲阳联合加入Meta超级智能实验室担任联席负责人。
Daniel Gross
丹尼尔·格罗斯
AI Product Lead, Meta; Co-founder, Safe Superintelligence; Co-founder, C2 Investments
AI Insider Builder-Investor
AI Agents AI Infrastructure Enterprise AI
Born in Jerusalem in 1991 to American parents, Daniel Gross grew up in the Katamon neighborhood and embodies the archetype of the precociously entrepreneurial immigrant: he applied to Y Combinator at age 18 on "a whim," founded the search engine Greplin (later Cue) as a teenager, and sold it to Apple for a reported $40–60 million at age 22. He then led Apple's AI and search projects from 2013 to 2017 — giving him rare access to production-scale machine learning before the current generation of AI tools existed.
中文摘要
格罗斯1991年生于耶路撒冷，父母为美国人，在卡塔蒙社区长大，是典型的早熟移民创业者：他18岁"冲动"申请了YC，十几岁时创立了搜索引擎Greplin（后更名为Cue），22岁以据报4000至6000万美元出售给苹果。此后，他于2013至2017年主导苹果的AI与搜索项目，在当前AI工具诞生之前便积累了罕见的生产级机器学习实战经验。
As a Y Combinator partner from 2017 to 2021, Gross created the dedicated "YC AI" programme and launched Pioneer, a global early-stage accelerator. With Nat Friedman, he built C2 Investments and the Andromeda Cluster. His venture bets include pre-launch positions in Uber, Instacart, Figma, Rippling, Airtable, GitHub, and early-stage stakes in CoreWeave and Perplexity AI. He was named to TIME's 100 Most Influential People in AI (2023). In June 2024, he co-founded Safe Superintelligence (SSI) with former OpenAI Chief Scientist Ilya Sutskever — a stealth safety-focused AI lab — before departing in mid-2025 to join Meta's AI product division.
中文摘要
格罗斯于2017至2021年担任Y Combinator合伙人，创立了专属的"YC AI"项目并发起了Pioneer全球早期加速器。与弗里德曼共同构建了C2 Investments与仙女座集群。其风险投资押注包括Uber、Instacart、Figma、Rippling、Airtable、GitHub的早期股份，以及CoreWeave和Perplexity AI的早期投资。他入选《时代》2023年百位最具影响力AI人物。2024年6月，他与前OpenAI首席科学家伊尔亚·苏茨克弗联合创立了Safe Superintelligence（SSI），专注于AI安全研究；2025年中期离开SSI，加入Meta AI产品部门。

Sonya Huang
黄颂茹
Partner, Sequoia Capital
VC · Growth AI Applications
Generative AI AI SaaS AI Infrastructure
Sonya Huang trained neural networks on brain scans and astrophysics data as an undergraduate, combining an economics education with hands-on CS and ML exposure. She worked as an investment banking analyst at Goldman Sachs and as a private equity associate at TPG Global before joining Sequoia Capital, where she has been a General Partner since September 2018. At Sequoia, she focuses on the growth team's AI and data/infrastructure portfolio. Her academic and professional formation — economics training for market analysis, computer science for technical differentiation, and ML fluency for trajectory assessment — made her one of Sequoia's sharpest evaluators of AI opportunity.
中文摘要
黄颂茹在本科阶段便已在脑部扫描与天体物理数据上训练神经网络，将经济学教育与计算机科学及机器学习的实践经验相结合。她曾任高盛投资银行分析师及TPG全球私募股权助理，此后加入红杉资本，自2018年9月起担任普通合伙人。在红杉，她专注于增长团队的AI与数据/基础设施投资组合。其学术与职业背景——经济学训练用于市场分析、计算机科学用于技术差异化评估、机器学习流畅度用于趋势判断——使她成为红杉最敏锐的AI机会评估者之一。
Huang led or co-led Sequoia's investments in OpenAI, Hugging Face, LangChain, Mercury, Streamlit, Glean, Gong, and Fireworks AI. She co-authored Sequoia's seminal 2022 report "Generative AI: A Creative New World" — among the first major VC frameworks to coherently map the generative AI stack — which became required strategic reading across Silicon Valley. She holds board positions at Mercury, Fireworks AI, Gong, and LangChain, hosts Sequoia's "Training Data" AI podcast, and co-organises the annual AI Ascent conference. In 2025, she appeared on the Midas List of top VC investors.
中文摘要
黄颂茹主导或联合主导了红杉对OpenAI、Hugging Face、LangChain、Mercury、Streamlit、Glean、Gong及Fireworks AI的投资。她联合撰写了红杉2022年里程碑式报告《生成式AI：一个富有创造力的新世界》——这是最早系统梳理生成式AI技术栈的主流VC框架之一，迅速成为硅谷战略必读文本。她在Mercury、Fireworks AI、Gong和LangChain担任董事会成员，主持红杉"训练数据"AI播客，并联合组织年度AI Ascent大会。2025年，她荣登Midas榜。

Solo Capitalist, Gil Capital; Author, High Growth Handbook

Elad Gil holds a PhD from MIT in biology/mathematics and spent formative years at Google, where he started Google's mobile team, was involved in the Android acquisition, and was the original PM for Google Mobile Maps — products that collectively reached billions of users. He joined Twitter via the acquisition of his startup MixerLabs (GeoAPI), subsequently serving as VP of Corporate Strategy. He later co-founded Color Genomics, a genomic health company backed by T. Rowe Price and General Catalyst. This background — researcher, product operator, founder, and executive — is unusually broad for a solo investor.
中文摘要
伊莱德·吉尔持有麻省理工学院生物学/数学博士学位，早年在谷歌担任要职——创建了谷歌移动团队、参与了Android收购，并担任谷歌移动地图的首位产品经理，相关产品后来触达数十亿用户。他通过其创业公司MixerLabs被收购而加入Twitter，后出任企业战略副总裁。他还联合创立了基因组健康公司Color Genomics，获T. Rowe Price和General Catalyst投资。这种研究员、产品运营者、创始人与高管的复合背景，对于一位单独VC而言极为罕见。
Operating as CEO of Gil Capital, Elad Gil has backed over 40 unicorns, including Airbnb, Coinbase, Stripe, Square, Figma, Airtable, Anduril, Gusto, Instacart, Notion, Pinterest, Retool, and Rippling. In the AI wave, he was an early investor in Perplexity AI, Character.AI, Mistral, Harvey, Pika, Anthropic, Scale AI, and Inflection. He co-hosts the "No Priors" podcast with Sarah Guo and is the author of the widely-cited High Growth Handbook. In 2025, he was reported to be raising a $1.5 billion fund and has identified AI-powered roll-ups (acquiring traditional businesses and transforming them with AI) as his next thesis.
中文摘要
作为Gil Capital的CEO，伊莱德·吉尔已投资超过40家独角兽企业，包括Airbnb、Coinbase、Stripe、Square、Figma、Airtable、Anduril、Gusto、Instacart、Notion、Pinterest、Retool和Rippling。在本轮AI浪潮中，他是Perplexity AI、Character.AI、Mistral、Harvey、Pika、Anthropic、Scale AI及Inflection的早期投资人。他与郭莎拉联合主持"No Priors"播客，并著有广受引用的《高增长手册》。2025年，据报道他正在募集15亿美元基金，并将AI驱动的并购整合（收购传统企业并以AI改造之）确定为其下一阶段投资逻辑。
Vinod Khosla
维诺德·科斯拉
Founder, Khosla Ventures; Co-founder, Sun Microsystems
Contrarian VC Silicon Valley Legend
Biotech / Robotics AI Labor Disruption Clean Energy
Born in Pune, India in 1955, Vinod Khosla holds an MS in Biomedical Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University and an MBA from Stanford GSB. At 27, he co-founded Sun Microsystems in 1982 with Andy Bechtolsheim — pioneering open systems and RISC processors. Within five years Sun reached $1 billion in annual revenue, a then-extraordinary growth curve. Tobias Lütke was born in Koblenz, Germany in 1980. A gifted programmer from childhood — he began modifying computer hardware and rewriting game code at age 11 — he completed a programming apprenticeship at the Carl-Benz-School after tenth grade rather than pursuing a university degree. In 2002, he moved to Canada to be closer to his future wife. Unable to find work without a formal degree, he built his own e-commerce platform using Ruby on Rails, initially to run an online snowboard shop called Snowdevil (2004). This platform became the foundation for Shopify, launched in 2006.
中文摘要
托比·吕特克1980年生于德国科布伦茨，自幼编程天赋异禀——11岁便开始修改电脑硬件和重写游戏代码。他在完成十年级学业后选择在卡尔-奔驰学校接受编程学徒培训，而非攻读大学学位。2002年，他为了与未来的妻子相聚而移居加拿大。因无正式学历难以求职，他用Ruby on Rails自行构建了一套电商平台，最初用于运营名为Snowdevil的在线滑雪板商店（2004年）。这套平台后来成为Shopify的基础，于2006年正式推出。
Under Lütke's leadership, Shopify went public in 2015 and grew to become one of Canada's most valuable technology companies. He retains a 7% stake with a 40% voting interest via a dual-class share structure, giving him firm control of the company's strategic direction. His net worth was approximately $12.3 billion as of August 2025. He is a core contributor to the Ruby on Rails framework and the creator of Active Merchant, a widely used open-source payment library. He joined the Coinbase board in 2022. His AI commentary — particularly his recent public statements that AI is eliminating routine work and requiring all Shopify employees to demonstrate that tasks cannot be done by AI before requesting additional headcount — are closely watched as signals of how AI is reshaping enterprise operations.

在吕特克的领导下，Shopify于2015年上市，成长为加拿大最有价值的科技公司之一。他通过双重股权结构持有7%的股份，并保有40%的投票权，对公司战略方向拥有绝对控制。其净资产截至2025年8月约为123亿美元。他是Ruby on Rails框架的核心贡献者及广泛使用的开源支付库Active Merchant的创建者，并于2022年加入Coinbase董事会。他的AI评论——尤其是近期公开表示AI正在消除重复性工作，并要求Shopify员工在申请新增人手前先证明该工作无法由AI完成——被密切关注为AI重塑企业运营的重要信号。
Sir Demis Hassabis

Drug Discovery AGI Research Protein Folding
Demis Hassabis was born on July 27, 1976, in London, to a Greek Cypriot father and Chinese Singaporean mother. A child prodigy in chess, he reached chess master status at age 13 — competing against adults in international competitions. He earned a double first-class degree in Computer Science from Cambridge University (1997), then founded the videogame company Elixir Studios, producing award-winning titles for publishers including Vivendi Universal. He returned to academia to complete a PhD in Cognitive Neuroscience at University College London (2009), followed by postdoctoral research at MIT and Harvard. His unique intellectual trajectory — chess grandmaster-level pattern recognition, game design, neuroscience, and computer science — proved to be precisely the combination needed to build DeepMind.
中文摘要
哈萨比斯1976年7月27日生于伦敦，父亲为希腊裔塞浦路斯人，母亲为新加坡华裔。他是国际象棋天才少年，13岁即达到国际象棋大师级别，参加成人国际赛事。他以优等双学位荣誉毕业于剑桥大学计算机科学系（1997年），随后创立了电子游戏公司Elixir Studios，为Vivendi Universal等出版商制作获奖游戏。他后回归学术界，在伦敦大学学院完成了认知神经科学博士学位（2009年），并在麻省理工学院和哈佛大学从事博士后研究。其独特的知识轨迹——棋手级模式识别、游戏设计、神经科学与计算机科学的融合——恰恰是创建DeepMind所需的精确组合。
Hassabis co-founded DeepMind in London in 2010 with Shane Legg and Mustafa Suleyman. Google acquired DeepMind in 2014 for over $500 million. Under his leadership, DeepMind produced a series of milestone achievements: the Deep Q-Network (2013) that learned to play Atari games at superhuman level; AlphaGo (2016), which defeated world champion Lee Sedol 4–1 in the ancient board game of Go, widely considered AI's "Sputnik moment"; and AlphaFold 2 (2020), which solved the 50-year-old protein structure prediction problem with accuracy competitive with experimental methods — a breakthrough with immense implications for drug discovery and biology. In 2024, Hassabis and John Jumper were awarded the Nobel Prize in Chemistry for AlphaFold — the first time the Nobel Committee formally recognised an AI researcher. He was knighted for his contributions to science and was named a UK Government AI Adviser. He also co-founded Isomorphic Labs, a commercial AI drug discovery spinoff.
中文摘要
哈萨比斯于2010年在伦敦与谢恩·莱格和穆斯塔法·苏莱曼联合创立了DeepMind。谷歌于2014年以逾5亿美元收购DeepMind。在其领导下，DeepMind取得了一系列里程碑式成就：以超人类水平学习Atari游戏的深度Q网络（2013年）；以4:1击败围棋世界冠军李世石的AlphaGo（2016年），被广泛视为AI领域的"斯普特尼克时刻"；以及以堪比实验方法的精度解决了50年悬而未决的蛋白质结构预测问题的AlphaFold 2（2020年）——这一突破对药物发现和生物学具有深远意义。2024年，哈萨比斯与约翰·朱珀因AlphaFold荣获诺贝尔化学奖——这是诺贝尔委员会首次正式表彰AI研究人员。他因对科学的贡献而被授予爵位，并被任命为英国政府AI顾问，同时联合创立了商业AI药物发现公司Isomorphic Labs。
